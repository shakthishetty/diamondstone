Our Story

Born from the expertise and integrity of Majdi Kiwan, Diamond Stone blends centuries-old craftsmanship with modern precision to deliver premium Omani marble worldwide. Anchored in values of excellence and trust, we craft authentic, timeless beauty—building a brand as enduring as the stones we source.
